About

Right from junior school, I have been a top ranker in my class and was the Junior School House Captain of Delhi Public School, Digboi. I was enrolled in the Science Stream with Computer Science as my elective and graduated with 96% in the AISSCE examinations. I have a starry eyed fascination for technology. Business leaders like Steve Jobs and Bill Gates, who stood at crossroads of technology and business, have always been inspirational to me. Craving to understand the technological rationale of these businesses, I pursued a Bachelors Degree in Computer Engineering at Delhi Technological University (Formerly DCE).

DTU helped me dive into the deep abyss of computer science which opened up even broader perspectives for me.To mould my innovative mindset, I joined research labs at City, University of London and University of Padova. Currently, in pursuit of a swift transition to the managerial front of technology, I am joining Barclays as a Risk Analyst. Also, I have secured admission in the revered Masters in Management programme at London Business School and will be hopefully joining in 2021.
